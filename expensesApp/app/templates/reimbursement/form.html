üí° How Reimbursements Work

Reimbursements track money received back from returned items. When you record a reimbursement:

  • The amount is subtracted from the account of the person who received it
  • The original shared expense is not affected (it stays split)
  • Other participants must pay the reimbursed person their share

Example:

  • Alice buys a table for $100 (shared with Bob)
  • Alice's share: $50, Bob's share: $50
  • Alice returns the table, gets $100 back
  • Alice records a $100 reimbursement to herself
  • Settlement: Bob owes Alice $100 (his $50 + her $50)
